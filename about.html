About Cheftastic

Cheftastic is a platform for you to share your favourite recipes for the world to see and rate them. You can also explore recipes from your favourite chefs and give them a thumbs up.
No need to care about your diet, With cheftasstic you can check the calories you are taking along with other details you might need to know before started cooking. Search from over thousand recipes and choose your best dinner for tonight.

FEATURES
  • Get step-by-step instructions on how to cook every single recipe with detailed ingredients list.
  • With the help of search bar , search through the related recipe list by specifying a search term.
  • Save recipe or Bookmark your favourite recipes in Saved.
  • Share your favourite recipes with friends via any social networking sites.
  • Post your recipes to your Cheftastic Feed.
  • Receive notifications when someone likes your recipe.
